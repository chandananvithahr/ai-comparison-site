## Synthesia Review 2025

**Synthesia** is the enterprise leader in AI avatar videos. Fortune 500 companies use it to create training, onboarding, and compliance videos at scale without hiring camera crews.

## What Synthesia Does

You write a script, choose an AI avatar from 230+ options, pick a language, and Synthesia generates a professional talking-head video in minutes. The avatar lip-syncs accurately to the audio.

No camera. No studio. No lighting. No post-production.

## Key Features

- **230+ AI avatars** — diverse, professional, customizable
- **130+ languages** with natural-sounding synthesis
- **SCORM export** — compatible with all major LMS platforms
- **Custom avatars** — create an avatar from your own likeness (Enterprise)
- **Templates** — pre-designed layouts for common business video types
- **Team collaboration** — multi-user projects with version control
- **Video translation** — convert existing videos to other languages

## Pros

- Most diverse avatar library (230+)
- SCORM export for e-learning platforms
- Enterprise-grade collaboration features
- Fastest time-to-video for presenter-style content
- 130+ language support is genuinely impressive

## Cons

- Avatars can look artificial at close range
- More expensive than HeyGen for similar quality
- Limited creative customization vs traditional video
- Free tier is almost unusable (3 demo videos total)

## Rating: 4.4/5

**Best for:** Corporate training departments, L&D teams, HR departments, e-learning content creators, companies needing multilingual video at scale.

## Vs HeyGen

The main competition is HeyGen. Synthesia wins on enterprise features and avatar diversity. HeyGen wins on avatar realism and video personalization. For pure enterprise L&D: Synthesia. For marketing/sales: HeyGen.

## Verdict

Synthesia is the gold standard for enterprise AI video. If you're creating corporate training content, it's the most efficient tool available. For individual creators, HeyGen is usually a better fit.
