## Captions AI Review 2025

**Captions AI** is the best auto-captioning app for creators who record themselves. Its key differentiator isn't just captions — it's the AI **eye contact correction** feature that genuinely changes how your videos look.

## The Eye Contact Feature

Most creators who record with a script or teleprompter look slightly off-camera. Their eyes drift down or to the side. Captions AI's AI analyzes your video and **digitally shifts your pupils** to face directly into the camera.

The result looks natural. Viewers get the feeling of direct eye contact even when you were reading from a script. This alone is worth the subscription for many creators.

## Key Features

- **Auto-captions** — 95%+ accuracy in English, multiple languages supported
- **AI eye contact correction** — looks natural, makes a real difference
- **Filler word removal** — automatically removes "um," "uh," "you know"
- **Caption styles** — animated styles that match current TikTok trends
- **Video editing** — basic cuts, trim, B-roll support
- **Mobile-first** — iOS app with desktop web app
