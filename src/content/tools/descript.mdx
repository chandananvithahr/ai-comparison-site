## Descript Review 2025

**Descript** is not an AI video generator — it's an AI video editor that works completely differently from every other tool on the market. Instead of a timeline, you edit a transcript.

## The Core Concept

When you upload a video to Descript, it transcribes every word. You see the transcript on the left, the video on the right. To delete an "um" or cut a rambling section, you just **select the text and hit delete**. The video updates automatically.

This is revolutionary for non-editors. Your podcast producer, marketing manager, or VA can now edit video without learning Premiere Pro or Final Cut.

## Key Features

- **Overdub:** Clone your voice. Record corrections in your own voice without re-recording the video.
- **AI filler word removal:** One click removes all "um," "uh," "you know," "like" from your video.
- **Screen recording:** Built-in screen recorder with automatic transcript.
- **Collaboration:** Multiple team members can edit the same project.
- **Multi-track:** Handles A/V sync for interview-style podcasts.

## Pros

- Text-based editing is genuinely revolutionary
- AI filler word removal saves hours
- Overdub voice cloning is surprisingly accurate
- Non-editors can use it immediately
- Great for podcasts and talking-head content

## Cons

- Not for generative AI video (can't create from prompts)
- Export quality can be inconsistent at lower settings
- Collaboration features still maturing
- Overdub quality varies by voice

## Rating: 4.4/5

**Best for:** Podcasters, YouTubers doing talking-head content, content marketing teams, anyone who hates timeline-based editing.

## Verdict

Descript is one of the most genuinely innovative products in the video space. If you produce any talking-head content or podcasts, it will save you hours every week. It's not for everyone, but for its target use case, it's unbeatable.
