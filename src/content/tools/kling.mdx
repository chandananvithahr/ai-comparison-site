## Overview

Kling AI, developed by Kuaishou Technology, has quickly emerged as one of the most compelling AI video generators available. Its standout strengths are its realistic human motion simulation and its remarkably generous free tier — 66 credits refreshed daily.

## Who is Kling For?

Kling is ideal for creators who prioritize realistic human and physical motion in their videos, budget-conscious creators who want to explore AI video without paying upfront, and social media creators who want natural-looking video content.

## Key Features

**Kling 1.6 Model:** The latest model from Kling produces video with impressive realism, particularly in human movement. Athletes running, people dancing, characters interacting — Kling's physics engine handles these scenarios better than most competitors.

**Daily Free Credits:** With 66 credits refreshed every 24 hours on the free tier, Kling is the most accessible AI video generator for experimentation. This is a significant advantage over Runway's one-time 125 credit allotment.

**Virtual Try-On:** A unique feature that allows you to visualize clothing items on models — useful for fashion and e-commerce brands.

## Verdict

Kling is an exceptional value proposition, especially on its free tier. For creators focused on realistic scenarios featuring people and everyday physics, Kling frequently produces results that match or exceed Runway's output.
